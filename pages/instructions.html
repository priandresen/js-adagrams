How to Play ADAGRAMS

  1. Click the "Draw Letters" button to draw 10 random letter tiles.
  2. Use the drawn letters to form the highest scoring word possible.
  3. Type your word into the input box and click "Submit Word".
  4. Your score will be calculated based on the letters used in your word.
  5. The game consists of 5 rounds. Try to achieve the highest total score!

Good luck and have fun playing ADAGRAMS!
